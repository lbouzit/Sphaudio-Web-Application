Sphaudio

Welcome to Sphaudio! This application allows you to select a song playlist, and then experience the music through our stunning visualizer.

First, go to our homepage at alexkharrison.com/sphaudio.html. Next, build your playlist from our wide selection of music. Simply highlight the songs that you wish to add!

Next, you get to personalize your visualizer. Using the "Navigate" tool bar, go to the "Customization" page. There are several visualizer themes for you to choose from. Select the theme that you'd like, and then hit "Save Changes."

Now, you are ready to use the visualizer! Clicking "Save Changes" on the customization page will automatically take you to the visualizer page. Hit play to begin your playlist!
